authorizer

The scalable, flexible, and fast permission and access-management platform.

Flexible Permission Models

ABAC, RBAC, and ACL permission models are all supported. Hierarchical permission models are also supported. Take a look at the Examples.

Built to Scale

Inspired by Google Zanzibar - "Google's Consistent, Global Authorization System", the Authorizer platform is built with scale in mind. Learn more about the Architecture.

High Performance

The platform is optimized to reduce tail latency with a variety of clever tricks. The goal is to support a 99th percentile latency SLA of 120ms or less. See the Performance documentation for more info.
